How it works (quick)
  • Add up to 3 centroids. They appear below as sections on the canvas.
  • Drag cards from Unassigned onto a centroid; they stay where you drop them.
  • Drag within/across sections to reposition/reassign. Drop back on Unassigned to unassign.
  • Restart moves all cards back to Unassigned. Reset clears and re-seeds “Jessica”.
